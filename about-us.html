About Us

Students club CODING AND DESIGN was inaugurated by Prof. G. Raja Rao on 15-09-2015. On this occasion, he emphasized the importance of college clubs for the professional growth of engineers. He added that the students shall make use of this platform and expose their talents and innovative abilities in the field of Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE). This club is formed to improve the coding knowledge. On this inaugural ceremony, each and everyone involved in coding and design Club have participated.
